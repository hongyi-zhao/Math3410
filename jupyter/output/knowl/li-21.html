Suppose \(U\subseteq W\text{,}\) and let \(B=\{\vec{u}_1,\ldots, \vec{u}_k\}\) be a basis for \(U\text{.}\) Since \(B\) is a basis, it's independent. And since \(B\subseteq U\) and \(U\subseteq W\text{,}\) \(B\subseteq W\text{.}\) Thus, \(B\) is an independent subset of \(W\text{,}\) and since any basis of \(W\) spans \(W\text{,}\) we know that \(\dim U = k \leq \dim W\text{,}\) by TheoremÂ 2.4.1.
