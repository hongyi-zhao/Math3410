Theorem 2.2.5.

Let \(V\) be a vector space, and let \(\vec{v}_1,\ldots, \vec{v}_k\in V\text{.}\) If \(\vec{u}\in \spn\{\vec{v}_1,\ldots, \vec{v}_k\}\text{,}\) then

\begin{equation*} \spn\{\vec{u},\vec{v}_1,\ldots, \vec{v}_k\} = \spn\{\vec{v}_1,\ldots, \vec{v}_k\}\text{.} \end{equation*}
